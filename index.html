Rizwan Ur Rehman Sagar, PhD

Postdoctoral Research Fellow

Tsinghua University, Shenzhen

Rizwan Ur Rehman Sagar completed his MSc (2005–2008) degree in Physics from University of Sargodha, Pakistan, MPhil (2008–2010) degree in Physics from COMSATS University Pakistan, PhD (2010–2015) degree in Materials Science and Engineering, Tsinghua University, Beijing, China. He has been served as post-doctorate fellow in College of Materials Science and Engineering, Shenzhen University. During that period, he was awarded research funding from Postdoctoral Science Foundation of China (No.2016M592531).

Recently, he is serving Tsinghua University, Shenzhen, as a post-doctorate and obtain a prestigious research funding from National Natural Science Foundation (11850410427), China. He is actively engaged in the fabrication of low-dimensional carbon-based materials and detected interesting physical phenomena involved therein. He has been published more than 30 scientific articles with a google h-index ~ 11 in well-reputed journals such as CARBON, ACS Applied Materials and Interfaces, Sensors and Actuators, etc.
