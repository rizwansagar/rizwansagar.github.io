Rizwan Ur Rehman Sagar, PhD

Associate Profesor

Jiangxi University of Science and Technology, China

Rizwan Ur Rehman Sagar completed his MSc (2005–2008) degree in Physics from University of Sargodha, Pakistan, MPhil (2008–2010) degree in Physics from COMSATS University Pakistan, PhD (2010–2015) degree in Materials Science and Engineering, Tsinghua University, Beijing, China. He has been served as post-doctorate fellow (2016-2018) in College of Materials Science and Engineering, Shenzhen University. During that period, he was awarded research funding from Postdoctoral Science Foundation of China (No.2016M592531). In addition, he has been selected as an excelent postdoc (2018-2020) from Tsinghua Shenzhen International Graduate School, Shenzhen, China and and obtain a prestigious research funding from the National Natural Science Foundation (11850410427).

Recently, he is serving Jiangxi University of Science and Technology, Ganzhou, as Associate Professor China. He is actively engaged in the fabrication of low-dimensional carbon-based materials and detected interesting physical phenomena involved therein. He has been published more than 30 scientific articles with a google h-index ~ 11 in well-reputed journals such as CARBON, Nano Research, ACS Applied Materials and Interfaces, Nanoscale, Applied Materials Today, Sensors and Actuators: A-B, and so on.

Interests

  • Flexible electro/magnetoelectronic devices
